# Pig_Game

##
GAME RULES:

- The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
- In each turn, a player rolls two dices as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score
- BUT, if the player rolls a 1 in either one of dices, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
- The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn
- The first player to reach input points on GLOBAL score wins the game, default winning score is 100
